Frequently asked questions about pricing

Everything you need to know before choosing your condominium management package.

What is the average condominium manager fee in Montreal?
In Greater Montreal, rates typically range from $25 to $50 per unit per month depending on the size of the corporation, included services and building complexity. Our packages start at $25/unit/month (Essential) and include administrative and financial services as well as Bill 16 compliance. A personalized quote is offered free of charge after a needs assessment.
Are there hidden fees in your packages?
No hidden fees. All our packages are fixed monthly fee with a clearly defined scope of services in the contract. The only possible additional fees are out-of-scope services requested on an ad hoc basis by the corporation (complex legal dispute, major renovation project, technical study), always communicated and approved in advance.
What is the minimum contract term?
Our management mandates follow industry standard practice in Quebec: annual renewable contract, with a 60-day termination clause in case of dissatisfaction. No penalty for non-renewal at term. We believe in loyalty through service quality, not through restrictive contracts.
Is the rate degressive based on the number of units?
Yes. The per-unit rate decreases progressively with the size of the corporation, since some fixed costs (accounting, AGM, declaration of co-ownership) do not grow linearly with the number of fractions. For example, a 60-unit corporation benefits from a lower unit rate than a 15-unit corporation, for equivalent services.
Can we change packages during the mandate?
Absolutely. You can upgrade at any time (e.g. Essential → Serenity) if your needs evolve: new Bill 16 work, growth of the corporation, loss of a volunteer board member, etc. A downgrade applies at the contract's anniversary date. The rate adjustment is prorated.
What exactly does 24/7 emergency service include?
An emergency line available 24/7, including nights, weekends and holidays. It covers critical situations: water damage, elevator breakdown, loss of heating in winter, intrusion, major power outage, etc. We immediately coordinate partner contractors to limit damage. Included in the Serenity and Custom packages.
