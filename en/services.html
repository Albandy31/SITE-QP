Our four pillars of service

A complete range of services to meet every need, with the responsiveness that sets us apart.

01

Administrative & legal

  • Organization and running of general meetings
  • Scheduling and chairing board of directors meetings
  • Updating and managing the declaration of co-ownership and the unit owners registry
  • Management and renewal of the building insurance policy
  • Preparation of annual REQ filings
  • Monitoring of Bill 16 compliance obligations
  • Hiring and payroll for employees engaged by the corporation
02

Financial & accounting

  • Preparation and monitoring of the annual budget in close collaboration with the board
  • Collection of condo fees, follow-up on arrears and secure deposits
  • Payment of suppliers and cash flow management
  • Production of annual financial statements and tax filings for the corporation
  • Provision of required documents to the notary at unit sales
  • Planning and monitoring of the contingency fund in line with the Bill 16 study
  • Monthly bank reconciliation
03

Technical management & maintenance

  • Coordination of periodic inspections and upkeep of the mandatory maintenance log
  • Negotiation and management of maintenance contracts
  • Oversight of major and minor work: tendering, contractor selection, supervision
  • Emergency service available 24/7 for any breakdown or incident
  • Coordination of insurance claims
  • Regular inspection reports and proactive recommendations to anticipate issues.
  • Annual inspection of fire alarms and other systems
  • Coordination of interior and exterior work
04

Communication & governance

  • Proactive communication with all unit owners
  • Handling of requests and complaints — response guaranteed <24h
  • Promotion of transparency and unit owner participation
  • Mediation and conflict resolution
  • Software platform available to all unit owners

Frequently asked questions about our services

Answers to the questions most often raised by condominium board members.

What condominium management services do you offer?
We cover the four pillars of management: administrative and legal (general meetings, registers, declaration of co-ownership, insurance), financial (budgets, contributions, contingency fund), technical (maintenance, RFPs, project supervision) and communication (private portal, newsletter, complaint handling). All our packages include 24/7 emergency service.
Do you offer 24/7 emergency response?
Yes. An emergency line is active 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, for water damage, elevator breakdowns, major mechanical failures or any incident affecting occupant safety. A manager dispatches a qualified contractor and coordinates the intervention end to end.
Which areas do you serve?
We serve the entire Greater Montreal area: Montreal Island, Laval, Longueuil, South Shore (Brossard, Saint-Lambert, Boucherville) and North Shore (Terrebonne, Blainville, Repentigny). We consider any request within a reasonable radius.
Can I select only some services (à la carte management)?
Absolutely. In addition to full management, we offer à la carte modules: running a general meeting, preparing the annual budget, handling an insurance claim, achieving Law 16 compliance, or one-time support for self-managed corporations.
How long does the transition to Quatre Piliers take?
A typical transition takes 30 to 60 days. It includes recovering files, taking over the accounting, updating the register of co-owners, communicating with residents and a kickoff meeting with your board of directors.
