Bill 16: are you compliant?

Bill 16 imposes new obligations on every condominium corporation in Quebec. At Quatre Piliers, we guide you step by step.

Mandatory maintenance logCondominium corporations must produce a maintenance log by August 14, 2028.
Contingency fund studyCorporations have until August 14, 2028 to obtain their first contingency fund study.
Corporation attestationMandatory at every unit sale since 2025.
